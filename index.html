The Conference

VIT Bhopal University, in collaboration with The International Society for Air Breathing Engines (ISABE), is hosting the First International Conference on Sustainable Aerospace Technologies and Innovations on February 7-8, 2025. This conference will focus on the latest advancements in green aviation, carbon-neutral propulsion systems, and strategies to mitigate environmental impacts in avionics, aerodynamics, and propulsion technologies.

The aim of this conference is to provide a forum for visionaries, researchers, and industry leaders to converge and to shape the future of aerospace technology. Join us as we collaborate to define a more sustainable future for aerospace, where innovation meets responsibility.

Some of the key highlights: -

  • Invited Keynote Sessions
  • Research Presentations
  • Poster Presentations
  • Technical Student Competition
  • Industry & Academia talks on Conference Theme
  • Dr.G. Viswanathan Best Poster Presentation Award
  • Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Best Paper Research Award
  • One-Day Workshop on Gas Turbine Propulsion

About VIT Bhopal University

The VIT legacy, founded 38 years ago by Dr. G. Viswanathan, began with Vellore Engineering College and continues to thrive under his leadership as Chancellor, along with Vice President Mr. Sankar Viswanathan and Assistant Vice President Ms. Kadhambari S. Viswanathan. VIT Bhopal, established in 2017 on a 300-acre ultra-modern campus in Sehore, aims to become a global education hub. The university offers core engineering programs like Computer Science, Mechanical, Aerospace, and Electronics, alongside allied subjects.

VIT Bhopal's curriculum emphasizes innovation through applied learning, with features like the Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS), Project-Based Learning (PBL), and advanced laboratories. The campus provides unlimited internet access, mandatory soft skills training, and an Entrepreneurship Cell to support student development. Various clubs and career development cells address extracurricular and employment needs.

School of Mechanical Engineering

The School of Mechanical Engineering (SMEC) at VIT Bhopal offers undergraduate and research programs in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. Established at the university's inception, SMEC features faculty with specializations in areas like Machine Design, Thermal & Fluid Sciences, Manufacturing Engineering, Propulsion, Aerodynamics, and Structures. The school provides core and elective courses designed in consultation with industry experts.

Faculty members are actively engaged in research and consultancy across various fields, including Acoustics, Energy, Vibration Control, CFD, Non-destructive Testing, and Aerospace Structures. SMEC employs a student-focused teaching method, CALTech, to enhance learning and spark research interest. The school is equipped with state-of-the-art labs and offers opportunities for students to develop soft skills and professional abilities aligned with industry demands.
