Conference Themes

Academia Members (Faculty) / Research Scholars/ Research Centres & Establishments/ Scientists from India and Abroad are invited to submit their original research work in the topics mentioned here

The conference theme is not limited to above mentioned. Any research work relating to other areas of Aerospace Engineering are also heartily welcomed.

All research papers should present novel and/or innovative viewpoints within the general scope of the conference's themes. Every submission will undergo a plagiarism check using Turnitin software.

*Only presented papers at the conference with similarity scores below 15%, will proceed for formal review for publication. Online presentations will be subjected to approvals from the committee (Registration is Mandatory)

*The top 10 work during the ICSATI -2025, will be accepted for ISABE Conference 2026.
